1962:\nAfter the discovery of Soviet medium-range ballistic missile bases in Cuba, tensions rise between the East and West. After attempts to stabilize the situation fail, the Soviet Union retaliates by pushing it's forces into West Germany.\n\nSoviet naval forces still rely on relatively obsolete surface warships, but cruise missile submarines are a threat to NATO convoys.\n\nDifficulty: Hard\nSonar is generally poor and torpedoes must be used at short range to be effective.
